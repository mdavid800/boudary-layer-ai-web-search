A key nuance is that **Seagreen Phase 1 as built is 1,075 MW with 114 turbines**, while the historical **Alpha & Bravo** consent envelope and some databases may show different capacities or turbine counts; those relate to earlier consented layouts or later extensions such as **Seagreen 1A**, not the built operational Phase 1 asset.
