**Note:**  
There is a discrepancy in reported turbine count and capacity between some databases (e.g., EMODnet and EuroWindWakes list 75 turbines and 1,140 MW), but all official project and OEM sources confirm 114 turbines and 1,075 MW as the as-built, operational figures for Seagreen Phase 1. The 1,140 MW figure refers to the consented/planned capacity, but the as-built export capacity is 1,075 MW.
