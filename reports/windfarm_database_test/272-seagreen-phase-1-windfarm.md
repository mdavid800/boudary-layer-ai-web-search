A key nuance is that some databases still show **1,140 MW and 114 × 10 MW** from the original turbine order, but current owner/operator sources describe the commissioned Seagreen Phase 1 project as **1,075 MW with 114 turbines**; likewise, public sources do not clearly confirm a single as-built hub-height figure, and care is needed not to confuse this built phase with later Seagreen extension proposals.
