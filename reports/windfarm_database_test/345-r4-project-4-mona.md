A key nuance is that **Mona remains a DCO-envelope project**: capacity is fixed at up to 1,500 MW, but turbine model, OEM, exact rating, and final foundation choice are **not yet fixed** in current public sources; the published rotor diameter, hub height, and turbine count are **maximum design parameters**, not as-built specifications.
