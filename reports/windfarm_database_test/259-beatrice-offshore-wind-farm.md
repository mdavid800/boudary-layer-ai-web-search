The main nuance is that some online datasets mix the **commercial 588 MW wind farm** with the much earlier **Beatrice demonstrator**; that likely explains the incorrect 2009 commissioning context and some turbine-dimension discrepancies. For the built 84-turbine project, the strongest current evidence supports **Siemens SWT-7.0-154 turbines, 84 units, 588 MW total, jacket foundations, operational since 2019**.
