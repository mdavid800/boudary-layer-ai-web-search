A key nuance is that publicly accessible sources clearly confirm the **gross installed capacity** of 160 MW for Horns Rev I, but a separately stated **MEC/export-capacity** figure was not found; this profile therefore does not assume MEC equals installed capacity. Historic ownership is also affected by Danish utility restructuring: the project was developed by **Elsam** but sits today in **Ørsted’s** operating portfolio.
