This profile assesses the **Morven offshore wind farm project in Scotland’s INTOG leasing round**, developed by **Caledonia Offshore Wind Farm Ltd (Ocean Winds + Mainstream Renewable Power)**; it is distinct from other UK “Morven” uses and remains a pre-consent development project.
