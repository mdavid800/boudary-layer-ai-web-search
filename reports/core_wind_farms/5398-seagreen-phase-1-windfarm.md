A key nuance is that older planning and database entries often show **114 turbines x 10 MW = 1,140 MW**, but current owner/OEM sources for the as-built project consistently state **1,075 MW** and **114 turbines**; accordingly, the official built project appears to be 114 Vestas 10 MW-class turbines operated at a lower net/gross project rating than the simple nameplate sum.
