The main ambiguity is between the **earlier consented Seagreen Alpha/Bravo concept** and the **as-built Seagreen Phase 1 / Seagreen 1**. Current official sources support **114 turbines and 1,075 MW installed capacity**, not the older **75 turbines / 1,140 MW** figures. A second nuance is hub height: official project material cites **107 m above LAT**, while some databases list **120 m**, likely due to a different datum or earlier design basis.
