A key nuance is that **Horns Rev I** is the original 160 MW project; some web sources discuss the broader Horns Rev area alongside **Horns Rev 2** and **Horns Rev 3**, so capacity, owners, and dates should be kept phase-specific. For this early project, installed capacity is well evidenced, but a distinct project-specific MEC figure and a dated FID are not clearly disclosed in current public sources.
