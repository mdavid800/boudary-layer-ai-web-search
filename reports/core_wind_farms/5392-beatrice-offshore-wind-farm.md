A key nuance is that some datasets confuse the operational **Beatrice Offshore Wind Farm** with the much earlier **Beatrice Demonstrator Project**; that likely explains the erroneous 2009-style commissioning signal in secondary turbine databases. There is also a hub-height conflict: some datasets list 101 m, but project/industry sources for the built 84-turbine commercial farm more commonly report **105 m**.
