An important nuance is that **capacity and turbine count reflect different layers of project definition**: the original DCO/government record supports **up to 1,800 MW** and **up to 158 turbines**, while RWE’s current live project pages now present Boreas as a **1.4 GW** project within the wider 4.2 GW Norfolk Zone.
